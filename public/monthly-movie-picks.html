Brandon's Monthly Pick for Best Movie

October 2023 Pick

The Land Before Time

This one is a classic kids movie that any child born in the eighties or nineties will find nostalgic and endearing. Bonus points if you can name five dinosaurs!
1.Little Foot
2.Cera
3.Spike
4.Duckie
5.Petrie

November 2023 Pick

The Big Lebowski

This is a wacky movie with with a great cast of characters who are all insane in their own quirky, fantastic way. Hilarity ensues in a twisted tail of Mistaken Identities, Rug Ruining, Kidnapping, Ransom, and Bowling.

December 2023 Pick

Violent Night

As is in keeping with the season, I've chosen the Christmas movie of this year to be Violent Night. David Harbour does a great impression of a battle-hardened-turned to booze, magical Santa Claus. This is a movie that is campy enough to enjoy while staying away from being self aware. "Merry Christmas, Ya Filthy Animal"!

January 2024 Pick

Once Upon A Time In Hollywood

This is an amzing film by Quentin Tarantino. he has recreated the 60's in an homage to decadence and depravity, with pulls from real history and unique twists that leave you captivated through a rollercaoster of emotions.
